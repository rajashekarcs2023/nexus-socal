Event Description: 

Join the UBS × Product Space × Fetch.ai AI Agent Challenge (4/4-4/10) and build an AI agent that turns user intent into action – from helping students find internships to exploring the future of human-AI collaboration or planning financial decisions.

Your solution will run through ASI:One and Agentverse, demonstrating how AI agents can execute tools and solve real-world problems.

Your solution will run through ASI:One (a platform for building and running AI agents) and Agentverse (a platform for discovering, connecting, and deploying agents), demonstrating how AI agents can execute tools and solve real-world problems.

Teams can consist of 1–4 people. Selected finalists will present their agents live to a panel, and the top ____ teams will receive prizes. Technical workshops will also be held throughout the competition and are open to all participants.

Social Media Blurb/Company Description:

Fetch.ai is a Cambridge, UK and Menlo Park, CA based AI company, building the foundational infrastructure for the emerging agent economy. Fetch.ai enables autonomous, goal-oriented AI agents to discover, coordinate, and transact on behalf of users, businesses, and devices across an open, decentralized digital ecosystem. Its full-stack platform spans consumer, developer, and enterprise use cases, including ASI:One, a personal agentic AI users own and customize; Agentverse, a global discovery and monetization layer for AI agents; and Fetch Business, which allows companies to deploy verified, always-on brand agents. Together, these products make the agent-based web discoverable, interoperable, and economically viable, powering the next generation of intelligent applications.
For more information, visit fetch.ai.

General Prompt: Identify a problem at UCLA and create an AI Agent that aims to solve this issue.

Participants’ solutions must:
Create an AI agent and register it on Agentverse
Be discoverable and usable through ASI:One
Turn user intent into real, executable outcomes

Requirements to be eligible for a prize:

Develop a single or multi-agent orchestration that demonstrates reasoning, tool execution, and solves a real-world problem.
Use any agentic framework like Claude SDK, OpenAI Agent SDK, Google ADK, Langgraph, CrewAI, etc. of your choice OR simple plain python to bring your idea to life.
Register your agents with Agentverse and implement the Chat Protocol (mandatory) to support direct ASI:One interactions.
No custom frontend is required - the use case must be demonstrated directly through ASI:One
