## Tips for Best Results

💡 **Be descriptive:** Include details about appearance, clothing, expression, and personality

💡 **Set the mood:** Mention the style (realistic, cartoon, fantasy, sci-fi)

💡 **Describe the setting:** Context helps create better characters (e.g., "in a futuristic city")

💡 **Think visually:** Focus on what the character looks like, not their backstory
