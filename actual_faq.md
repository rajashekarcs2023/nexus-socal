What is this event?
The UBS × Product Space × Fetch.ai AI Agent Challenge is a week-long competition where you’ll build an AI agent that turns user intent into real-world actions.

Who can participate?
All UCLA students are welcome to join. No prior experience with AI agents is required.

Can I work alone or do I need a team?
You can participate solo or in a team of up to 4 people.

When is registration?
Registration opens Monday, 3/30 and closes Friday, 4/3 at 11:59PM.

When does the challenge start?
The prompt will be released on Saturday, 4/4, and the build period runs until Friday, 4/10.

How much time should I expect to spend?
This is a flexible, asynchronous challenge – most teams are expected to spend a few hours across the week.

When are final presentations?
Finalists will present on Monday, 4/20, 6:00–8:30 PM PST.

What do we have to build?
You’ll build an AI agent that solves a real problem at UCLA and turns user intent into actionable outcomes.

Do we have to follow the suggested topics?
No – they’re just inspiration. You’re free to explore any idea that fits the prompt.

What platforms/tools do we need to use?
Your solution must:
Be registered on Agentverse
Work through ASI:One
Support the Chat Protocol

Do we need to build a full app or UI?
No – no custom frontend is required. Your solution should be demonstrated through ASI:One.

Will there be workshops?
Yes – technical workshops will be hosted during the challenge to help you get started.
What do we need to submit?
You’ll submit:
ASI:One shared chat link (demo of your agent)
Agentverse agent URL

How are projects evaluated?
Projects are evaluated based on:
Real-world usefulness
Agent reasoning + execution
Technical implementation
Creativity

Where can I find resources?
Starter pack: https://innovationlab.fetch.ai/resources/docs/intro

Who do I contact if I have questions?
Reach out to maggieju@g.ucla.edu and harryhu@g.ucla.edu.
